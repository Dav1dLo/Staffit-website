Staff-IT Data Privacy Policy

Introduction

Staff-IT ("Staff-IT," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of your personal data. This Data Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy") outlines how Staff-IT collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your personal data in the context of our recruitment activities. By engaging with our services or providing your personal data to us, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and consent to the processing of your personal data as described herein.

1. Personal Data Collection

We collect personal data directly from you as well as from other available sources to the extent permitted by applicable law. The categories of personal data typically collected and processed during the recruitment process include but are not limited to:

​

  1. Information you provide: When you apply for a position with Staff-IT or interact with us through an agency or third party, we may collect information such as your name, address, contact details, competences, skills, experience, education, CV or resume, references, and preferred methods of contact.

​

  1. Technical Information: When you access our online services, our servers may automatically record your IP address, access times, pages visited, browser type, and other technical information. We may also receive additional information from telecommunications operators when you use our services over telecommunications networks.

​

  1. Other Information: We may collect information from public sources and, where relevant for a specific assignment or client requirement, conduct background checks only when this is necessary and proportionate for the recruitment process. This may include situations where (i) the nature of the role or assignment requires it, (ii) a client explicitly requests it as part of the selection process, and/or (iii) we are required to do so under applicable law. Where required, we will inform you in advance and, if applicable, request your consent.

2. Use of Personal Data

Staff-IT will process your personal data for the following purposes:

​

  1. Contracting Activities: To evaluate your qualifications for IT freelance jobs and assess your suitability for available assignments.

​

  1. Communication: To contact you regarding contracting activities, provide information about available IT freelance job vacancies, and request additional information when necessary.

​

  1. Proactive Freelance Job Search: Our recruiters may proactively search for new freelance job opportunities based on the information provided in your resume, in line with your preferences and qualifications.

​

  1. Compliance: To comply with applicable legal obligations and industry standards.

3. Data Retention

Staff-IT retains personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy and in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

​

  1. Active profile / contracting relationship: Personal data is retained for as long as your profile remains active and/or while Staff-IT is in contact with you regarding current or potential IT freelance assignments.

​

  1. Inactive profiles: If there has been no interaction or activity related to your profile for a period of 24 months, your personal data will be deleted or anonymized, unless retention is required to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or defend legal claims.

​

  1. After the contracting relationship ends: Once you are no longer connected with Staff-IT, your personal data will be retained in accordance with the retention periods described above, unless a longer retention period is required under applicable law.

​

This retention approach is without prejudice to your rights as described in this Privacy Policy.

4. Data Security

Staff-IT takes data security and protection seriously. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

5. Data Transfers

By providing your personal data to Staff-IT, you acknowledge and consent to the transfer and processing of your data worldwide, including in countries that may not provide the same level of data protection as your home country.

Your personal data may be shared only with the following categories of recipients, to the extent necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy:

​

  1. Clients for whom IT freelance assignments are being considered.

​

  1. Recruitment or contracting partners and agencies acting on behalf of Staff-IT.

​

  1. Service providers supporting our IT systems, hosting, communication, and administrative services.

​

  1. Public authorities or legal bodies, where required to comply with applicable law or legal obligations.

​

Staff-IT does not sell your personal data and does not share your personal data with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes unrelated to contracting activities.

6. Your Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal data, which include:

​

  1. Access: You may request access to your personal data and information about how we process it.

​

  1. Rectification: You have the right to request corrections to inaccuracies or incomplete personal data.

​

  1. Erasure: You may request the deletion of your personal data, for example where:

​

  • The personal data is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.

​

  • You have withdrawn your consent and there is no other legal basis for processing.

​

  • You have successfully objected to the processing of your personal data.
    This right may be limited where Staff-IT is required to retain certain data to comply with legal obligations or to establish, exercise, or defend legal claims.

​

  1. Objection: You can object to the processing of your personal data in certain situations.

​

  1. Withdrawal of Consent: If we process your personal data based on your consent, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time. Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before such withdrawal.

​

7. Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy or the processing of your personal data, please contact us at:

​

Staff-IT Email: info@staffit.be

Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. Any updates will be posted on our website with the effective date. Please review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect and process your personal data.
